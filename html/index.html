Meet The Doctor

Brenon Farmer, DDS

Dr. Farmer and our staff really take the time to listen to you, and we explain everything we are going to do before it happens. That way, you are completely comfortable. Beyond that, we do all we can to ensure pain-free dentistry during every procedure. So much so, that patients have said that Dr. Farmer’s chair is a comfortable place to be.

The care at Countryside Dental extends past your appointment though. During your first visit, you will receive expert advice on how to care for your teeth in between visits, and after any procedure, you can be sure that Dr. Farmer will call to check on you, going above and beyond to provide you with the care you need.

Dr. Farmer offers general dentistry as well as orthodontics, dentures, implants, and if further treatment is needed he is readily able to refer you to a specialist.
